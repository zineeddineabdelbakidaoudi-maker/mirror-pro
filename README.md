## 📖 About The Project

**MiroirPro** is a specialized Desktop ERP crafted specifically for manufacturing workshops dealing with mirrors and armoires. It handles the complete lifecycle from raw material stock and production tracking to the final Point of Sale (POS) transaction. 

> *Note: For intellectual property protection, certain core logic modules, proprietary algorithms, and database files have been intentionally omitted. This repository showcases the architectural design and capabilities of the software.*

## ✨ Key Features

- **📊 Smart Dashboard:** Track monthly revenue, pending orders, stock alerts, and debt warnings at a glance.
- **🛠️ Production Lifecycle:** Manage orders through states: *Draft → Confirmed → Production → Ready → Delivered*.
- **📦 Stock Management:** Raw material CRUD operations, reservations, and low-stock alerts.
- **⚖️ Inventory Auditing:** Conduct physical count sessions with automated stock adjustments.
- **🧾 Islamic Finance Module:** Built-in Zakat calculator that aggregates cash, stock value, and receivables while deducting debts.
- **🖨️ Thermal Printing:** ESC/POS support (USB/Network/PDF) for instant receipts.

## 🛠️ Technology Stack

- **Core Language:** Python 3.12
- **UI Framework:** PySide6 (Qt6)
- **Database:** SQLite with SQLAlchemy 2.0+
- **Data Visualization:** Matplotlib
- **Packaging:** PyInstaller

## 💡 The Notion Behind MiroirPro

Manufacturing workshops often suffer from disjointed tools—using spreadsheets for stock, paper for orders, and separate apps for accounting. The notion behind MiroirPro is **"Unified Workshop Command"**. It brings every aspect of a mirror/armoire business under one roof, ensuring that when an order enters production, raw materials are automatically reserved, and when a sale completes, financial metrics are instantly updated.
